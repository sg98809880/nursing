Who Can Apply?

Check Your Eligibility

Most Indian nurses are eligible. Here's what you need to qualify for Germany's nursing recognition (Berufsanerkennung):

  • GNM (3-year) or B.Sc Nursing or M.Sc Nursing degree from a recognised institution
  • Valid registration with State Nursing Council or INC
  • Minimum 12 months of clinical work experience (preferred)
  • Willingness to learn German (B2 level required for licensure)
  • Age ideally between 21–38 years at time of application
  • No major criminal record; clean professional standing
